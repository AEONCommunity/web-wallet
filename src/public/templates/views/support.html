Who are we and how is AEON Pocket funded?

We are developers and work in our spare time on various open source projects to widen our knowledge. AEON Pocket is one of these adventures we embarked on. Currently we are funding it ourselves and all services provided on AEON Pocket is currently free. Fee charged during transaction is miner's fee. To know more about us you can visit our team page on github.
